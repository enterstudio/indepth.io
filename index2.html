Why In-depth?

In-depth inc was founded on values and ethos in common with the cultural sector. We value openness, collaboration, and the spread of knowledge.
Read more…

We are a Public Benefit Corporation, we answer to our values, not shareholders. Our data is open, our source code is visible, our work is collaborative. We are here to help cultural institutes grow, expand and improve, and through the cultural sector help all of us learn from our past, benefit in the present, and grow our future.

The Experience

The museum experience has been growing and expanding at an astounding rate, new technologies, new exhibits, new ways to explore and understand our history and prepare for our future.
Read more…

The In-depth platform allows museums to easily enrich their visitors experience through new digital technology. In-depth provides a base platform that engages visitors, adds a wealth of knowledge and creates additional ways for visitors to understand and consume exhibits.

We provide a toolkit to create digital experiences, allowing museums to focus on what makes them unique and keeps millions of visitors coming back each year.

Through an open and collaborative plugin system a museum can launch a ‘general’ museum app containing a searchable, location aware database and API of their collection. Then with In-depth’s free plugins create virtual reality, augmented reality and AI driven interactive digital experiences. For an even more customised experience, museum’s can write their own plugins to do just about anything!

The In-depth platform gives you the ability to build almost anything, without having to reinvent the wheel!

Openness

In-depth inc is was founded on and built around openness and collaboration. Our foundation document sets out our ethos for years to come with a focus on putting people before profit, openness over secrets and collaboration over competition.
Read more…

All our source code is visible, and can be modified and contributed to by users of our platform. Our plugins are completely open source (MIT Licence) and can be changed, modified, forked and even sold.

Our data is freely available, any collection information put online is visible to the world to read, copy, re-use and modify under creative commons, while photos and material that require copyright is provided under fair-use rules for education. We provide APIs, mass data download, syncing with 3rd parties and for museum’s the ability to access the entire database for any purpose.

We are never gatekeepers but we are reviewers. We curate the In-depth ecosystem, and reach out and assist those who need it, promote amazing contributions, and build upon the ideas from the world at large.

Collaboration is important, and we collaborate with all, museums, visitors and other businesses. Through our AlphaLabs program anyone can see and comment on the technology we are experimenting with. This feedback at a very early stage allows us to continue to build and expand our product shaped by what our customers and your visitors have to say.

We hate the idea of limiting what we’ve built from being used to it’s fullest and richest. That’s why we structure our licence and fees to allow micro museums (or anyone!) to use our product free. As a museum grows they slowly move from free to fee-paying based on the number of visitors and the usage of the In-depth platform by visitors.
